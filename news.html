Bi-Weekly Student Research Progress Meetings in the Climate & Health Lab.


December 9, 2025

The Climate & Health Lab holds bi-weekly research meetings every other Thursday, bringing together the Principal Investigator, and graduate researchers. During these sessions, students present brief updates on their projects, covering research objectives, data collection progress, preliminary analyses, challenges, and plans for the next two weeks. Each meeting features multiple presentations followed by open discussions where lab members critique methods, exchange ideas, and propose new directions.
